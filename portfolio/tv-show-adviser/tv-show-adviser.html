TV Show adviser.

Get popular show.

_This application is made with ReactJS.

_This project advised you about some good TV show thanks to the TMDB API.

_The background is the actual most popular TV show on TMBD. There are the title, the rank from the TMBD user voting and the synopsis of the show

Get suggestions.

_The list in the bottom of the app as suggestions for you.

_If you just arrived, suggestions are based on the popular TV show, but at the moment you click on one of the suggestion, it will be based on it.

Get a show you like.

_You can directly search a show by is name. The app will display the first element in the result from the database

_The suggestions will be then based on this research and give you equivalent shows.
